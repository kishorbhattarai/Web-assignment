Google will now let you automatically delete location and activity history. Here's how

Article By Eli Blumenthal, Jessica Dolcourt May 2, 2019

A small win in the fight for more privacy control. Google is letting users take back a little bit more of their privacy.On Wednesday, the search giant introduced a new autodelete feature for location, app and web history. As opposed to manually having to wipe their histories, with the new feature Google users will be able pick a length of time, either three months or 18 months, that Google will save the data. Any older data will be deleted automatically.
